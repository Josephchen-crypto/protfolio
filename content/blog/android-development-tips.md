# 10 Android Development Tips for Senior Engineers

After 12+ years of Android development, here are my key learnings:

1. **Always use Kotlin Coroutines** for async operations
2. **Implement proper error handling** - Don't swallow exceptions
3. **Use Jetpack Compose** for modern UI
4. **Follow Material Design 3** guidelines
5. **Write unit tests** - TDD approach
6. **Use dependency injection** - Hilt recommended
7. **Optimize for performance** - Profile before optimizing
8. **Secure your app** - Store sensitive data properly
9. **Monitor crashes** - Firebase Crashlytics
10. **Keep learning** - Android evolves fast

## Feature Comparison

| Feature | Recommendation | Priority |
