# My Journey to Becoming a Super Individual

I built and deployed this website in a single day on Cloudflare Pages. I'm thrilled to meet you through this blog and give you a deeper understanding of who I am.

## About Me

I was a senior Android application developer with over a decade of experience. In 2025, I made the decision to shift to remote work.

There were two main reasons. First, my child was just born, and I wanted to be present. Second, the domestic job market and work environment had come into direct conflict with both my family's needs and my physical health. The relentless pressure of being the sole person carrying nearly an entire team's workload, working overtime almost every day, had taken a serious toll on my body and strained my relationships. In late 2024, I finally decided to walk away from that life and begin my Super Individual plan.

## Work and Life

Right now, I work from home remotely to keep the household running while being fully present for my baby.

When I'm working, my child is looked after by his grandmother. Every hour, when I stand up to stretch, I go find my baby and play with him. When my wife gets home from work, I spend the evenings chatting with her — sharing the problems we each faced that day and the little wins that made us happy.

Most importantly, I've escaped the slow suicide of my previous job. I've protected my health so I can have a longer life to spend with the people I love.

## What's Next

I'll keep enriching the content on this website. I genuinely love this site — it looks incredible. But there's still so much to improve and build out. The process is both thrilling and exciting. It feels like raising another child, watching this website grow little by little.
